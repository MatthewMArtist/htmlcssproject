Unit 1, outcome 3, task 3.2 - Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency

Ways to improve productivity in business range from a more engaging work environment, more training on the part of the project members, better management of deadlines, contorting the project to fall within the project members skill sets and proper management and task assignment. These all improve productivity by enhancing the team members willingness to work regardless of their physical state, providing a guaranteed boost to productivity. Vitral to all of these are also an effective array of tracking methods, such as trello boards and project storage sites such as github.
