Unit 1, outcome 1.3 - Plan and carry out tasks using IT – e.g. making your website. How will you plan it? Include a link to your Trello board.

When planning and enacting the development of my website, I shall use the Trello website extensively by working on one of its trello boards. Within this I shall detail all future tasks, activities I’m working on completing as well as finished tasks, so that I understand exactly how the project stands at any time. This way, I will always be aware of what I need to do next and record all previous progress for future reference.

Furthermore, I will be using trello as a reference point to see what my written tasks are easily, checking it repeatedly in order to get a clear idea of what the task describes - Then store those tasks as completed once I finish them. [4]
