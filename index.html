About me

My name is Matthew Morris and I'm an artist who aspires to make his own game. I have spent two months learning to code and many years learning to draw in various styles. I decided to learn to code because I realised that I had the opportunity to make a unique and interesting gaming experience, and felt compelled not to ignore that urge. I intend on becoming effective in multiple languages so that when I initiate the beginning of my games project, I will be well versed in coding and be able to fulfill it to the constraints of my timeframe.
