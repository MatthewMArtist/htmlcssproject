Unit 2, outcome 1, task 1.6 - Describing access issues that need taking into account - describe issues that people with disabilities may face when using webpages and how we can overcome them.

To begin with, those with colour blindness will find themselves unable to distinguish some colours from others, meaning that certain combinations of them will be illegible from their perspective. Furthermore, it may be necessary to account for those with photosensitive epilepsy, and thus I would remove anything that might trigger a response in that disability. However, after analysing the requirements needed to adhere to the needs of other disabilities, I have deemed it cost prohibitive in time, skill and resources.
