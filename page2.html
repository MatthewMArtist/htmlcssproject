Unit 1, outcome 1, task 1.1 - Describing the purpose of IT in modern business.

IT within a business is a mechanism to provide It innovation. IT services enable faster decision making, more accurate analysis and planning, ease of use by employees and many other benefits to the efficiency of your business, compared to those that do not incorporate IT. [1]

This innovation can be provided through new and unique systems developed as a result of IT, such as email, inventory management systems, data management systems, AI algorithms and more. These all provide completely unique opportunities for improvement that are available only through the use of IT, making it a necessity in order for your business to compete in those areas. [2]
