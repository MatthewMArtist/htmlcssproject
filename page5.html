Unit 1, outcome 1, task 1.4 - Describe the risks that might impact digital projects â€“ what causes digital projects to fail? Why?

The first thing that could cause a digital project to fail is a lack of employees with the necessary skills. This means that they are unable to achieve certain aspects of the project that are vital to its success, inevitably leading to a failed result.

Another thing that could cause failure in such a project would be an incorrectly researched and executed planning phase that ends up asking for something totally unsuitable. This usually results in a team or single developer who, whilst following the plan, ends up submitting a sub-par finished product that does not meet the needs of the company requesting it.

A final reason that a digital project could fail is inadequate time given to the projects timeline in comparison to the resources available to be used. In this case, regardless of how hard the employees work on the project, they are unable to produce a finished one because the deadline is massively shorter than their skills and funds can abide by.
